Advanced Signal Filtering for Mean Reversion Trading

Xu, Firoozye, Koukorinis, Treleaven, Zhu (UCL, 2026) — SSRN 6225198

Develops a regime-aware mean-reversion framework using adaptive signal filters (LAFO) to determine latent fair price. The spot-filter spread yields buy/sell signals when price deviates beyond a threshold from the SMA-estimated fair value. Neural network filters (RNN, CNN, S4) approximate solutions within this framework. Our doob implementation uses the simplified SMA-based spread: δ = (close − SMA) / SMA; go long when δ < −threshold.

Early Birds Get the Vol: Morning Volatility Uncertainty and Variance Risk Premium

Bevilacqua, Hizmeri (U. Liverpool, 2026) — SSRN 6212458

Morning VVIX (10:00 EST) strongly predicts next-day variance asset returns due to limited attention and slow-moving beliefs. The signal achieves Sharpe ratios of 2.09–3.24 on VIX futures (long-only, 75th percentile threshold). Our doob adaptation uses daily VVIX close as a proxy, computing a rolling percentile to generate risk_off (long when VVIX low, calm regime) or contrarian (long when VVIX high, elevated uncertainty) signals on equity assets.

Methodology

Backtest Parameters and Notes

Execution Details

  • All backtests executed via doob run paper-research --output json
  • Capital: $1,000,000 per backtest
  • Fee model: IBKR Tiered (round-trip cost deducted per trade)
  • Train window: 2020-01-01 to 2024-12-31 (~5 years, ~1,260 sessions)
  • Test window: 2025-01-01 to 2026-03-29 (~1.25 years, ~315 sessions)
  • Price data: local parquet from ~/market-warehouse (split-adjusted, not dividend-adjusted)
  • VIX/VVIX data: local parquet at asset_class=volatility/symbol=VIX

  • SMA windows: 25, 50, 70, 90, 120, 160

  • VVIX thresholds: 0.5, 0.6, 0.7, 0.75, 0.8, 0.9

  • Total combinations: 5 x 5 x 6 x 2 = 300

Scoring

  • Score = 0.6 x Test Sharpe + 0.4 x Train Sharpe
  • Blended score weights out-of-sample performance more heavily to penalize overfitting
  • Top 20 overall (top 10 per rule) selected for the leaderboard
  • Equity curves generated for the overall top 10 using --include-audit

Caveats

  • adj_close == close in this warehouse (IB TRADES data). Buy-and-hold understates true total return by ~1.3%/yr due to missing dividends
  • VVIX proxy uses daily VIX close data, not intraday 10:00 EST VVIX as in the original paper
  • Mean reversion filter uses simple SMA spread, not the neural LAFO filter from the original paper
  • Results are not forward-looking: test window may include recent market regime shifts
